🤼 Kabaddi हिंदी Raid - कक्षा 4 🤼

How to Play:

🚩 Two teams take turns "raiding" with a Hindi grammar/vocabulary question (15 seconds per raid).

✅ Correct answer = Successful raid! Your team scores +2 points and tags 1 opponent player out.

❌ Wrong answer / time out = Raider caught! Opponent scores +1 point and your team loses 1 player.

🏆 Each team starts with 7 players. A team is ALL OUT when it has 0 players left — game over instantly!

⏱️ If nobody goes All Out, the game ends after 10 raids each — highest score wins.
